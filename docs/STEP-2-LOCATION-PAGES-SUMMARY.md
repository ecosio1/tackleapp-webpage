## Location-Specific Content

### Naples
- Focus: Inshore flats, Naples Pier, Gordon Pass, Rookery Bay
- Unique: Extensive backwater system, excellent redfish/snook

### Tampa
- Focus: Tampa Bay system, Skyway Pier, extensive flats
- Unique: World-famous pier, diverse bay ecosystem

### Miami
- Focus: Biscayne Bay flats, bonefish/permit, offshore Gulf Stream
- Unique: Legendary flats fishing, proximity to Gulf Stream

### Fort Myers
- Focus: Caloosahatchee River, Sanibel Causeway, backwaters
- Unique: River system, diverse freshwater to saltwater

### Sarasota
- Focus: Inshore flats, grass beds, mangrove shorelines
- Unique: Extensive shallow water opportunities
